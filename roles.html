Roles at Bluedot

Join Us

Whatever your role, business area or location, at Bluedot you'll get the chance to make a difference for yourself, our business, and people with emergency healthcare needs around the world.

We offer opportunities across various levels, in teams ranging from Flight physician and Control centre executive, to Operation manager and Case managment specialist. Join us in helping millions of people with critical healthcare needs.

Control Centre Executive

The Control Centre Executive plays a key role in coordinating air medical operations and supporting patients, partners, and internal teams in real time. This role involves managing communication, tracking missions, and ensuring smooth coordination between medical teams, flight operators, and ground services.
