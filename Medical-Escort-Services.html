When medical escort support is the right option

Many patients are fit to travel on commercial airlines but require medical support, supervision, or reassurance during the journey.

Medical escort services are designed for patients who can remain seated during travel but should not fly alone due to their medical condition, stage of recovery, age, or ongoing treatment needs.

This service enables safe travel on scheduled flights, with professional medical supervision provided from departure through arrival

How medical escort support works

Medical escort services focus on supervision, monitoring, and assistance ensuring the journey is safe, comfortable, and medically appropriate.

It begins with medical review

Before travel, our medical team reviews the patient’s condition to confirm suitability for escort-based travel. This includes assessing stability, mobility, medication requirements, and any in-flight considerations.

Then travel is coordinated

Once approved, we coordinate travel arrangements, airline notifications where required, and any medical equipment or medication needed during the flight.

Support throughout the journey

Patients are accompanied by qualified medical professionals such as doctors, or paramedics who provide monitoring, assistance, and reassurance throughout the flight.

Who medical escort services are for

Medical escort services are commonly used for:

  • Patients recovering from surgery or illness
  • Elderly patients requiring supervision during travel
  • Patients travelling for ongoing treatment or follow-up care
  • Individuals who should not fly unaccompanied for medical reasons

Choosing the right level of support

Medical escort services are suitable when patients are fit to sit and travel but require medical supervision. If a patient requires continuous monitoring, specialised equipment, or stretcher support, commercial airline stretcher or air ambulance options may be more appropriate. Our team can help assess and recommend the safest option.
