We are a global provider of air ambulance
and medical transport services, supporting
patients worldwide.

From air ambulance missions to commercial airline medical transfers, Bluedot exists to ensure that distance never stands in the way of life-saving care.

We support patients and families during some of their most critical moments, delivering medical transport by air across cities, countries, and continents with care, urgency, and purpose.

Vision

To set the global standard in air ambulance services, delivering accessible and safe medical transport driven by an unwavering commitment to patient care.

Mission

To provide world-class air medical transport worldwide, ensuring safe, efficient, and compassionate transfers through skilled teams, advanced medical technology, and trusted global partnerships.

Our Values

Excellence in Care

Delivering world-class medical and air transport services, with patient safety, dignity, and wellbeing at the core of everything we do.

Reliability & Trust

Maintaining the highest standards of dependability, so patients, families, and partners can rely on us at every critical moment.

Innovation & Progress

Continuously advancing medical transport through technology and progressive practices to improve care quality and efficiency.

Integrity & Transparency

Building trust through honest, ethical, and transparent communication, with accountability across all operations.
