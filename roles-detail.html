Control Centre Executive

Dubai / Regional Operations

At Bluedot, we provide global air ambulance and medical escort services, supporting patients, hospitals, insurers, and government entities across regions. Our operations are built around safety, clinical oversight, and precise coordination because every decision can impact a life.

Your mission

Bluedot is seeking a reliable and detail-oriented Control Centre Executive to support our 24/7 air medical operations. In this role, you will be central to coordinating missions, managing real-time communication, and ensuring smooth collaboration between medical teams, aviation partners, and ground services.

You will work in a high-responsibility environment where calm judgment, accuracy, and clear communication are essential. Your work will directly support safe, well-coordinated patient transfers.

Key responsibilities include

  • Coordinate air ambulance and medical transfer missions from initiation to completion
  • Act as the central communication point between medical teams, flight operators, ground handlers, and partners
  • Monitor flights and operational movements in real time
  • Support case documentation, reporting, and operational updates
  • Escalate issues promptly and support decision-making during time-sensitive situations
  • Ensure operational protocols, safety procedures, and service standards are followed
  • Maintain accurate records and support post-mission follow-ups

Skills & requirements

You'll bring these qualifications:

  • Experience in operations, coordination, aviation, healthcare, or assistance services
  • Strong communication skills and the ability to manage multiple tasks simultaneously
  • Ability to remain calm and organised under pressure
  • High attention to detail and a structured approach to work
  • Proficiency in basic systems, documentation, and reporting tools
  • Willingness to work shifts, including nights and weekends
